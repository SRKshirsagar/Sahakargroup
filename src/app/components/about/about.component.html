About Business

The SAHAKAR GROUP specialization is transportation of Full Truck Loads, ODC consignments and operations through Multi-modal services. Company has own fleet which operate for transportation of high-value cargo and has a large fleet of 250 attached trucks. COMPANY’S core team consists of trained professionals in the field of logistics, marketing, finance and operations, who are capable of handling challenging assignments in the most competitive environment. The Company considers human resource as the most valuable and vital asset and the personnel are trained continuously to upgrade their skills and knowledge-base to meet the challenges and demands of customers in a highly competitive milieu. Company understands customer’s specific requirements and suggests innovative and tailor-made solutions as the core team has the complete know-how and expertise to move goods of unconventional nature, goods that are required special care and attention. The parent company New Sahakar Roadlines has its strong network in South India. And is working for more than 25 successful years in the business with 100% assurance of safe and fast delivery of the consignment. N S Logistics founded in the year 2016 with the purpose of spreading the company’s network all over India. With increasing the branch and employee the company now has a strong network across all states in India.
